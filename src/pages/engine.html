Stirling Engine Project

The stirling engine is a term-long project that was originally intended to train students working in the machine shop on the lathe and the mill. Somewhere along the way, a Thermodynamics Professor (ENGS 25) adopted the project for his class. Since then, the stirling engine project has morphed into a rite of passage for the aspiring Dartmouth Engineer.
